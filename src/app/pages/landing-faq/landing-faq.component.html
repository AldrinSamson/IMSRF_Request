Frequently Asked Questions

GENERAL

 

Who is The Red Bank Foundation?

 

            The Red Bank Foundation is a non-governmental organization (NGO) in the Philippines that has a goal of delivering and supplying blood, as much as possible to all the needy people with no cost.

 

Is The Red Bank Foundation an organization authorized to collect and give blood?

 

Yes. The Red Bank Foundation offers services such as hosting bloodletting events for the Blood Donors, and dispatching blood requests to the individual Blood Requesters who are in need, without any cost.

 

How can you reach us?

 

            You can send message us through our email address: jst@redbank.org

 

 

REQUESTING FOR A BLOOD

 

How to request for blood?

              

You need to log in to your registered account to be able to proceed with requesting for a blood. A Request Form will be provided for you to fill-up. Your blood request will be validated by The Red Bank Foundation to make sure that the information you gave is real and not fake.

DONATING A BLOOD

 

 

How to donate blood?

 

            All you need to do is to participate in one of the Community-Based Mobile Blood Donation (CBMBD) of The Red Bank Foundation and register as a Blood Donor. You will be screened during the said bloodletting event, to know if you are qualified to donate blood.

 

The details of the upcoming CBMBD will also be posted here on the website.

 

Who can donate blood?

 

Blood Donors must be age 18 or older. Donors must weigh at least 110 pounds and be in good health. Additional eligibility criteria apply.

 

What are the qualifications before a donor can donate blood?

 

●     Weight: At least 110 lbs (50 kg).

●     Blood volume collected will depend mainly on your body weight.

●     Pulse rate: Between 60 and 100 beats/minute with regular rhythm.

●     Blood pressure: Between 90 and 160 systolic and 60 and 100 diastolic.

●     Hemoglobin: At least 125 g/L.  

 

How often can a person donate?

A healthy individual may donate every three months.

 

How long will it take to donate blood?

The whole process of blood donation, from the registration up to the recovery, will only take an average of 30 minutes. The blood extraction will take about 5-10 minutes. The blood volume will start replenishing within 24 hours.
